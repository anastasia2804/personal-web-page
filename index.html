About Me

Hi, my name is Anastasia Orlova and I am a full-stack web developer.

Born and raised in Russia, I have been living in the U.S. for the past 6 years. My professional background includes logistics, transportation and sales, as well as business development.

Curiosity for building things and bringing ideas to life led me to the Ironhack bootcamp, where I learned the fundamentals and best practices of modern web development. Besides programming, my interests include partner dancing, physical and mental fitness, traveling and being out in the nature.
